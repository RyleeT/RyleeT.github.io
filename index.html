About Me

I am an inquisitive and relentlessly determined computer science professional. I studied computer science at Southern New Hampshire University, where I obtained a Bachelor of Science degree while maintaining a 4.0 GPA. Throughout my studies and personal projects, I developed skills in data structures and algorithms, database creation and management, software engineering, software testing, team collaboration, security, and more. I completed numerous academic and personal projects like a real-time auction application, the backend for a messaging application, and a Java calculator. I took part in many group projects and applied frameworks like Scrum, which familiarized me with a team development environment. Throughout this educational journey, I also maintained an online storefront, where I researched, purchased, and managed inventory with the help of self-developed tools. This cultivated skills in automation, management, and customer interaction.

Prior to my education in computer science, I studied information technology with a specialization in software development. During this time, I learned the fundamentals of software engineering and the project development lifecycle. I performed numerous requirements analyses and system designs for mock companies, designed and managed SQL databases, developed Android applications, mapped out and developed authentication systems in Java, created student registration systems in C#, and more. This experience gave me great skills in designing software and databases, as well as communicating with stakeholders. Cumulatively, I believe my unique education puts me in a perfect position to hit the ground running as a full-fledged software engineering professional.
